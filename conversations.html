Meeting Someone for the First Time

Hello! I am John. Welcome to Tanzania!

üõí At the Market (Sokoni)

Shopping for Fruits
Customer:
Habari! Unauzaje maembe?
Hello! How much are you selling mangoes for?
Seller:
Habari! Maembe ni shilingi elfu mbili kwa kilo.
Hello! Mangoes are 2,000 shillings per kilo.
Customer:
Ni ghali sana! Unaweza kupunguza bei?
That's too expensive! Can you reduce the price?
Seller:
Sawa, shilingi elfu moja na mia tano. Bei ya mwisho.
Okay, 1,500 shillings. Final price.
Customer:
Sawa! Nipe kilo mbili tafadhali.
Okay! Give me two kilos please.
Seller:
Haya, shilingi elfu tatu. Asante kwa kununua!
Here you go, 3,000 shillings. Thanks for buying!
Tip: Bargaining (kupiga bei) is common and expected at markets in East Africa. Always start lower than your target price!
Bei gani?

Nataka kununua...
I want to buy...
Nipe...
Give me...

Ordering Food
Waiter:
Karibu! Mnapenda kuagiza nini?
Welcome! What would you like to order?
Customer:
Naomba menyu tafadhali.
May I have the menu please.
Waiter:
Hii hapa. Chakula cha leo ni pilau na kuku.
Here it is. Today's special is pilau with chicken.
Customer:
Vizuri! Nitaagiza hiyo. Na maji baridi.
Great! I'll order that. And cold water.
Waiter:
Sawa. Kitu kingine?
Okay. Anything else?
Customer:
Hapana, ni hayo tu. Asante!
No, that's all. Thank you!

üó∫Ô∏è Getting Directions (Maelekezo)

Finding Your Way
Tourist:
Samahani, hoteli iko wapi?
Excuse me, where is the hotel?
Local:
Hoteli? Nenda moja kwa moja, kisha geuka kulia.
The hotel? Go straight, then turn right.
Tourist:
Iko mbali?
Is it far?
Local:
Hapana, iko karibu. Dakika tano tu kwa miguu.
No, it's nearby. Just five minutes on foot.
Tourist:
Asante sana kwa msaada!
Thank you very much for the help!
Local:
Karibu! Safari njema!
You're welcome! Safe journey!

üöï Transportation (Usafiri)

Taking a Taxi
Passenger:
Hujambo! Unaweza kunipeleka uwanja wa ndege?
Hello! Can you take me to the airport?
Driver:
Ndio, ninaweza. Shilingi elfu ishirini.
Yes, I can. Twenty thousand shillings.
Passenger:
Sawa. Tutafika saa ngapi?
Okay. What time will we arrive?
Driver:
Kama dakika arobaini, kulingana na msongamano.
About forty minutes, depending on traffic.
Passenger:
Sawa, tuende!
Okay, let's go!
